Projects

Throughout my career and personal life, I have worked on various projects that have challenged me and allowed me to grow as a person. Some of my most significant projects include a student portal, where I had the opportunity to develop a solution that provides a hub for almost every need at the university.

Currently, I am working on a portal for recruitment, which is a place where you can submit job applications and get new opportunities. The portal covers the whole process of recruiting such as attracting candidates, selecting candidates, and offering the job. I am excited about this project because it has the potential to attract qualified people to work at the university.
